## One-Line Product Definition

`missless` upgrades link saving into durable knowledge and decision support. Given a link or paper, it extracts atomic claims and structured artifacts, aligns them with existing knowledge (`new|duplicate_of|equivalent_to|qualifies|contradicts|entails|extends`), and produces explainable read-priority recommendations with traceable source evidence segments.

## Core Product Promise

- Users should not need to re-read repetitive content to gain useful judgment.
- Every key conclusion must be backed by source evidence.
- Knowledge should compound over time through alignment and feedback.
