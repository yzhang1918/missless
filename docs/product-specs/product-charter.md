# Product Charter

Status: Active

## Problem

- Information overload and derivative content reduce signal-to-noise for decision making.
- Link collection alone does not produce durable understanding.
- Users need claims, methods, and evidence they can trust and revisit.

## Product Intent

`missless` is a judgment engine that converts sources into reusable knowledge objects and explicit decisions:
- what is new
- what is redundant
- what qualifies or contradicts prior knowledge
- what deserves deeper attention

## Durable Goals

- Convert source inputs into compact claims (Atoms) and structured outputs (Artifacts).
- Keep every important output auditable through evidence anchors.
- Make alignment decisions explicit and replayable.
- Keep human judgment in control of persistence boundaries.
- Compound knowledge quality over time through review and feedback.

## Core Principles

- Decision over collection.
- Citation is not destination.
- Extraction quality over interface assumptions.
- Evidence traceability over narrative fluency.
- Contracts and replayability over implicit behavior.
